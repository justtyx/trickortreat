Help trick or treating children collect candies within 30 seconds. And remember, you're not supposed to eat fruits on a Halloween night.

Each fruit or vegetable clicked, takes away one life. You will get 1 point for each candy and 2 points for polka-dotted candy.

You lose a game when the time runs out and you don't have 25 points, or if you lose all your hearts.
